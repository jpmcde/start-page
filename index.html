Welcome, New Students

For this class you'll need a gmail account.

  • If you don't a gmail account, make one then sign in to your new account.
  • Return to this page (http://icanlearn.today)
  • Click the period you're in and click on Request Access
  • Once you have access to our spreadsheet, click on the Schedules tab on the bottom of the screen
  • Add your name and put your program in like the others did
  • Click on the attendance tab and mark yourself P for today

Every day you come to class you'll come to this spreadsheet, take your attendance, then click on the tab with the day's date for the lesson

Our Projects

Blogging

Our class is learning to blog using Wordpress. We're learning everything from what to write to how to manage the backend and monitor comments as well as customize the blog to our liking.

Blogs

Presentations

We're learning how to use online collaboration tools to brainstorm and develop innovative presentations. Using tools like Prezi, students will learn how to create a presentation to inform, persuade, or engage their audience.

Presentations

Digital Storytelling

We're learning how to use sophisticated software to write and direct our own 3D animated movies. With a focus on screenwriting, students will learn how to create a Youtube channel and publish their movies to the web.

Virtual Worlds

Students will participate in a 3D immersive project where we will learn to design and build 3D learning simulations. Students will learn how to use 3D printing to make their 3D artifacts come to life.
