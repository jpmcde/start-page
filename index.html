Welcome, New Students

For this class you'll need a gmail account.

  • If you don't a gmail account, make one then sign in to your new account.
  • Return to this page (http://icanlearn.today)
  • Click the period you're in and click on Request Access
    • Once you have access to our spreadsheet, click on the Schedules tab on the bottom of the screen
  • Add your name and put your program in like the others did
  • Click on the attendance tab and mark yourself P for today

Every day you come to class you'll come to this spreadsheet, take your attendance, then click on the tab with the day's date for the lesson
